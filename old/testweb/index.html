A Great Brand is the Business What is your brand? Is it a logo or corporate ID as characterised in your letterheads, brochures and business cards? Or does it represent the very essence of your business? A good brand image sums up in a heartbeat just who you are, what you do, and most importantly, why it matters. It’s a standard that repeatedly confers unique benefits and reminds people why they like and trust you without having even to think about it. The mistake many companies make is to launch a new image without properly considering the commercial objectives. Then, relying on pretty pictures, friendly photographs and clichéd images they tell the world “Hey! We can’t tell you why we’re different but buy from us anyway – please”. Sure, striking visuals will look great at first; they may even be unlike anything else out there. But is it you? Is it, in the minds of your public, synonymous with your business and its innate qualities? If not, maybe it’s time to take a step back and consider how effective those brand images that speak so universally about you really are.
